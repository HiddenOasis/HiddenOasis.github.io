Services

Landscape

We plant the right material to accent your site and concurrently keep areas clean and modern. Beds are kept sharp with clean, hand dug edging. We offer maintenance to clients who wish to have their landscape detailed throughout the year. Our designs take into consideration the plants' needs concerning sunlight, water, size, and more.

Hardscape

Our portfolio of hardscapes includes retaining walls, pavers, fireplaces, and outdoor kitchens out of natural and man-made stone. With a sharp eye for aesthetics, our designs are functional and unique. Our kitchens and fireplaces are designed and built by us from the ground up. We love using natural stone for our patios and veneer when possible.

Lighting

We implement lighting to many of our designs not only for appearance but for safety as well. Lighting is an important aspect that will allow you to enjoy your oasis at night.

Water Features

Small waterfalls and ponds can add great ambiance to most any site. The sound of water is very relaxing and enjoyable. Whether its a small pond in the front yard, or a winding stream and waterfall in the back, Hidden Oasis can accent your property with pristine water features.

About

Hidden Oasis promises to design one-of-a-kind projects catered to your needs. From the material used to the methods by which they are installed, your oasis will be crafted with the utmost attention to detail. If you enjoy natural stone, beautiful water features, and solid wood structures, you have found the right company.

  • 1983-2005

    Our Humble Beginnings

    In 1983, Joel Geist started “Classic Touch Landscaping.” Through 2005, he grew the company from mowing apartment complexes to designing and building vast landscape, hardscape, water features, and wood structures. Joel's son, Chad Geist, grew up working beside his father when not in school.

  • 2006-2015

    Classic Design

    After rebranding to “Classic Design Landscaping,” Joel worked mainly with his son, Chad Geist. Together they completed various projects and designs. Chad went to college and ventured into a career in business. He would work with his father when time allowed.

  • 2016-Today

    Hidden Oasis

    Chad, wanting to work for himself, decided to start a landscaping company under his father's guidance. Hidden Oasis is the culmination of over 30 years of work in the landscaping industry. Joel and Chad work as a team designing and building for clients in Dayton, Columbus, and Northern Cincinnati areas.

Natural Stone

Travertine is great for outdoor use, especially around pools!

Available in pavers or tile, travetine contains many beautiful colors and natural movement. While we still build traditional concrete paver patios, we almost always recommend travertine. The stone cannot be fully appreciated until seen in person. We have never had a client regret using travertine over traditional pavers!

Not the color you want? Travertine comes in many varations of color and details.

Retaining Walls

Not only are retaining wall beautiful, they allow for intricate designs, raised beds, and function as a barrier to the earth behind them.

Retaining walls serve to "retain" the earth behind them. Their interlocking structure allows them to be built high while still serving their function. As seen in this picture, walls can be "cut" into hills to elegantly change the site.

Pergolas

Wooden structures provide great cover for patios and outdoor kitchens. We have access to large timbers necessary to build such sturdy edifices.

Pergolas can be designed to fit different needs and designs. Some may be just large enough to cover a small kitchen while others will provide shelter for large patios. Once again, our designs are custom-made to your needs.

Outdoor Kitchens

Outdooor kitchens are perfect for entertaining friends and family. Convenience meets aesthetics with the many cabinet and appliance options. Wow your guests the next time you host a gathering!

Outdoor kitchens truly make a statement. Their practicality is only exceeded by their style. Real stone veneer or man-made stone may be used to cover the outter wall of the kitchen. Cabinets and drawers are built in to the structure. Different size grills and sinks are available to meet our clients' preferences. We also love to add water features, one of which may be seen on the left side of this picture.

Natural Stone Steps

Natural stone steps complement many of our designs. The worn and natural look complements the tumbled pavers and walls.

Alongside natural steps, round river rock makes for a great alternative look to traditional dirt and mulch beds. We also design natural stone into our plans to help control water drainage.

Lighting

Lighting options are available for all manner of designs. However, with outdoor kitchens and pergolas, they can simply amaze.

Lighting provides safety as well as interesting atmospheres. From seeing steps and paths better, to finding your way around the kitchen, our lighting is designed to dazzle and delight!
