Services

Landscape

We plant the right material to accent your site and concurrently keep areas clean and modern. Beds are kept sharp with clean, hand dug edging. We offer maintenance to clients who wish to have their landscape detailed throughout the year. Our designs take into consideration the plants' needs concerning sunlight, water, size, and more.

Hardscape

Our portfolio of hardscapes includes retaining walls, pavers, fireplaces, and outdoor kitchens out of natural and man-made stone. With a sharp eye for aesthetics, our designs are functional and unique. Our kitchens and fireplaces are designed and built by us from the ground up. We love using natural stone for our patios and veneer when possible.

Water Features and lighting effects

Small waterfalls and ponds can add great ambiance to most any site. The sound of water is very relaxing and a very enjoyable. We implement lighting to many of our designs not only for appearance but for safety as well. Lighting is and important aspect that will allow you to enjoy your oasis at night.
